Today's Myth Buster-Eggs are bad for your heart

How? For most of us the cholesterol we eat-in eggs or any other food-doesn't have a huge impact on raising our blood cholesterol; the body simply compensates by manufacturing less cholesterol itself. The chief heart-disease culprits are "saturated and trans fats, which have much greater impact on raising blood cholesterol," notes Kris-Etherton.
